STEAM VISION is a vision for transforming the youth and humbly contributing
towards fulfilling the dreams of eminent leaders like Swami Vivekananda, who
said “If the student cannot come to the education, the education should go to
the student.” Dr. Abdul Kalam the missile man of India and the late president of
India said “Besides the normal curriculum the children should receive special
training towards developing 21st century skills.”

STEAM VISION was incepted in 2015 by Mr. Chander Malik who was inspired by
our Honorable Prime Minister Mr. Narendra Modi’s dream of a Digital India, Skill
India, Stand-up India and Make in India. STEAM VISION is an amalgamation of
Innovation, Entrepreneurship, Social outreach and Women empowerment. We
are a part of this innovation revolution.STEAM VISION took the initiative to
empower young students by imparting them the much needed 21st Century skills
through STEAM Education which is an educational approach to learning that
uses Science, Technology, Engineering, the Arts and Mathematics as access
points for guiding student inquiry, dialogue and critical thinking. It was our
passion to provide world class education which is Affordable,
Accessible and Acceptable. STEAM VISION believes that everybody has a right
to information.

    ecosystem to 754+ kids every day (We will achieve to provide this ecosystem to
    1021 + kids by 20th April 2020)
